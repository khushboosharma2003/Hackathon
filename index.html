ABOUT US
Our platform is an online integrated space designed for students from different universities and colleges. We believe that innovation is the key to improving education, and students invest significant effort into their academic projects. Our goal is to create a common knowledge-sharing platform that encourages collaboration while ensuring academic integrity through built-in plagiarism detection. Mission Statement: "To foster innovation and knowledge sharing among students, we aim to develop an integrated platform that facilitates collaborative project work at various academic levels, connecting technical and higher education institutions across the nation." This platform will serve as a valuable resource for the student community, enabling them to embark on unique and innovative projects while promoting academic excellence and integrity.
